How to Book a Appointment

Follow the Steps to Book an Appointment.

amp templates
  1. Tap Patient Login - If you Already registered, Login with your Credentials. If not already registered tap the register button to register yourself in Remember choose username and password wisely and also put in the details correctly.
  2. Tap Book Appointment - Once logged in, Tap Book Appointment to open the Appointment Form.
  3. Fill the form and tap on Book Appointment - Once the form is filled remember you can chose date only a month ahead of current date. If done correctly your appointment will be visible in the logged in state.
